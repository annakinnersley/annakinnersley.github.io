About Me

After writing various bits and bobs for sports websites, including 'TheSPORTBible', I decided to push myself further by working for my local papers.

I covered non-league football for over a year, which involved writing match reports and interviewing players/managers at the age of 17.

When the football season ended, I spent my summer after my A-Levels approaching different firms for work placements. I gathered work experience at CNNLondon, Daily Mail and talkSPORT. All of which required me to come up with quick, insightful, creative and informative articles to tight deadlines.

If that wasn't enough to whet my appetite, I am now undergoing an NCTJ-accredited Sports Journalism course at the University of Brighton.
